About the Reaction Tester Game

This mini-game challenges you to click the box as fast as possible after it randomly appears. Your reaction time is measured in seconds.

How it works:

  • A box appears on the screen at random times.
  • Your goal is to click it as fast as you can.
  • The game records your reaction time in seconds.
  • You can replay to improve your timing.
